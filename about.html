About Us

Founded in 2022, Toys 4 Humanity provides advocacy and support for less fortunate children across the Charlotte Metropolitan area all while making our greatest efforts to save the planet simultaneously. Our non-profit caters to children of all ages, but we will not have as much impact without the help of YOU! On another hand saving the earth sounds like a long-shot but a little effort goes a long way. The second part of our mission supports improving the enviroment by collecting trash and planting trees in protected forest across the Nation.

How Does This Work?

Purchasing one toy on our site will be sent to the child. Once the toy arrives to the child you will then be sent an images of the that reciving the toy that put a smile on their face. A portion of those proceeds are then be collected and used to plant trees in protected forest and build enviromental clean up teams that are dedicated to improving global warming.
